VIBGYOR 2K23

IMSEC Ghaziabad Organized Techno-Cultural Festival VIBGYOR 2023 on 4-5, May 2023. On Day One, 4th May, 11 different Technical Events were organized. Some of them are Blind coding, Build and win,Code War, BGMI, Robo war, Shark Tank and Innovative project demonstration.
More than 500 students participated in the events of their interest. Students showcased their talents, explored interests, and participated in a range of fun-filled events. Vibgyor was vibrant, colourful and energetic celebration of culture, creativity, and innovation. It was great memorable learning experience for students. they enjoyed a lot and made it a grand success!!
Vibgyor 2023 star night performance by Sukh-E Muzical Doctorz at IMSEC, Ghaziabad on 5th May!
Sukh-E Muzical Doctorz, the Punjabi singer enthralled the audience with his electrifying performance, and the crowd was left wanting more. With his unique blend of urban and desi beats, he had everyone grooving to his infectious tunes.
It was a night to remember, filled with non-stop entertainment and great music. The energy was high, and the spirit of youth was celebrated in all its glory.
