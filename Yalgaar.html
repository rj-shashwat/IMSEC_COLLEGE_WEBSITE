YALGAAR 2K23

YALGAAR," a LAN BattleGrounds Mobile India (BGMI) event organized by the Elite-E Gaming Club at IMSEC Ghaziabad, showcased intense gaming competition at the college level. With 49 teams participating, the event featured three rounds, divided into two groups ('A' and 'B') with distinct matches. In the climactic final match on Erangel, where both groups converged, Team Ziggle emerged as the champions by skillfully eliminating other squads. Team INF secured the 1st runner-up position, while Team Hyper claimed the 2nd runner-up spot. Hyper Monty of Team Hyper earned the coveted Most Valuable Player (MVP) title for his outstanding performance in whole three matches. "YALGAAR not only highlighted the gaming talent at IMSEC Ghaziabad but also provided a dynamic platform for BGMI enthusiasts to compete, fostering a vibrant gaming community within the college.
